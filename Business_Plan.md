# Business Plan: Stillnight Engine

## 1. Executive Summary

This document outlines the business plan for the development, launch, and growth of **Stillnight Engine**, a retro-style RPG with a unique Windows 3.1 aesthetic. Our mission is to deliver a high-quality, nostalgic, and strategically deep gaming experience for a targeted niche audience.

The product is a single-player, premium game that will be distributed digitally. The business model is a straightforward one-time purchase, priced competitively within the indie game market. We are committed to an ethical, customer-focused approach, with no microtransactions or intrusive monetization schemes.

Our target market consists of players who appreciate retro gaming, deep RPG mechanics, and unique artistic styles. We project modest but sustainable profitability, achieved through a lean development process and a targeted, grassroots marketing strategy. This plan details our strategy to successfully launch the Stillnight Engine and establish it as a recognized title within the indie RPG community.

## 2. Company Description

**Mission Statement:** To create unique, high-quality indie games that blend nostalgic aesthetics with modern, strategic gameplay. We are dedicated to ethical business practices and fostering a positive and engaged community around our products.

**Vision:** To become a recognized and respected name in the indie game development community, known for our creativity, craftsmanship, and commitment to our players.

**Core Values:**
*   **Creativity:** We are committed to developing innovative and imaginative games that stand out from the crowd.
*   **Quality:** We prioritize polish, stability, and a high-quality player experience.
*   **Community:** We believe in building a strong and supportive community around our games, and we are committed to open and transparent communication with our players.
*   **Integrity:** We conduct our business with honesty and integrity, and we are committed to ethical monetization and business practices.

## 3. Market Analysis

**Target Audience:**
*   **Primary:** Players aged 25-40 who grew up with classic JRPGs (e.g., early Final Fantasy, Dragon Quest) and early PC gaming (DOS, Windows 3.1/95). They are drawn to nostalgia, appreciate pixel art, and seek deep, strategic gameplay over flashy graphics. They are active on platforms like Reddit, Twitter, and specialized retro gaming forums.
*   **Secondary:** Younger indie game enthusiasts (aged 18-24) who enjoy unique aesthetics and challenging, non-mainstream RPGs. They are receptive to experimental art styles and are often discovered through platforms like itch.io, YouTube, and Twitch.

**Market Trends:**
The indie game market, particularly for retro-style RPGs, remains robust. There is a consistent demand for games that evoke nostalgia while incorporating modern design principles. The "pixel art" and "retro" tags are consistently popular on platforms like Steam and itch.io. The success of titles like *Undertale*, *Stardew Valley*, and *Sea of Stars* demonstrates a significant market for games that prioritize gameplay, art, and narrative over cutting-edge technology.

**Competitor Analysis:**
*   **Direct Competitors:** Games like *World of Horror* (similar retro UI aesthetic) and *Hylics* (surreal, unique art style) occupy a similar niche.
*   **Indirect Competitors:** Broader retro JRPGs like the *Saga* series or modern titles inspired by them.
*   **Competitive Advantage:** Stillnight Engine's key differentiator is its highly specific and faithfully executed Windows 3.1 aesthetic, combined with a deep, a data-driven creature collection and combat system. This unique blend of a familiar, nostalgic interface with complex RPG mechanics creates a distinct market position.

## 4. Organization and Management

**Team Structure:**
The project will be managed by a small, agile core team. We will adopt a flat organizational structure to encourage collaboration and rapid decision-making.

**Key Roles and Responsibilities:**
*   **Project Lead / Lead Developer:** Responsible for the overall project vision, core programming, and final decisions. Manages the development timeline and budget.
*   **Art and Design:** Responsible for all visual assets, including character sprites, UI elements, and environmental art. Ensures a consistent and high-quality aesthetic.
*   **Sound Design and Music:** Responsible for creating the soundtrack and all sound effects, aligning them with the game's retro theme.
*   **Marketing and Community Management:** Manages social media channels, press outreach, and community engagement. Gathers player feedback and manages the public-facing brand.

**External Resources:**
We will leverage freelance talent for specialized tasks as needed (e.g., promotional art, trailer creation) to maintain a lean core team and manage costs effectively.

## 5. Sales and Marketing Strategy

**Positioning:**
Stillnight Engine will be positioned as a premium, niche RPG for connoisseurs of retro aesthetics and deep, strategic gameplay. Our marketing message will emphasize its unique Windows 3.1 visual style, complex creature collection system, and its identity as a "modern retro" title.

**Pricing:**
*   **Launch Price:** $7.99 USD. This price is competitive within the indie RPG market on platforms like itch.io and Steam, offering significant value for the depth of content provided.
*   **Sales and Promotions:** We will participate in seasonal sales events on our distribution platforms (e.g., Steam Summer Sale, itch.io Winter Sale) with discounts of 25-50% to attract new players after the initial launch window.

**Distribution Channels:**
*   **Primary:** Itch.io will be our initial launch platform due to its favorable revenue share for developers and its strong community of indie game enthusiasts.
*   **Secondary:** A Steam release will be targeted post-launch, leveraging the platform's wider reach. This will require creating a compelling store page with a high-quality trailer, screenshots, and detailed descriptions.

**Marketing Activities:**
*   **Pre-Launch:**
    *   **Community Building:** Establish a presence on Twitter, Reddit (r/indiegames, r/retrogaming), and a dedicated Discord server. Share regular development updates, GIFs, and behind-the-scenes content to build a following.
    *   **Press Outreach:** Compile a list of indie game journalists, YouTubers, and Twitch streamers who cover similar niche/retro titles. Send out a press release and review keys closer to launch.
    *   **Demo/Beta:** Release a short, polished demo on itch.io to gather feedback and build a wishlist/mailing list.
*   **Launch:**
    *   **Launch Announcement:** Coordinated announcements across all social media channels, our Discord server, and through our mailing list.
    *   **Influencer Push:** Work with friendly YouTubers and streamers to create "Let's Play" videos and live streams during the launch week to maximize visibility.
*   **Post-Launch:**
    *   **Content Updates:** Support the game with regular bug fixes and periodic free content updates (new creatures, items, or small story events) to maintain community engagement and positive reviews.
    *   **Community Engagement:** Actively participate in our Discord and on social media, sharing fan creations, answering questions, and fostering a positive community.

## 6. Financial Projections

**Disclaimer:** The following projections are estimates based on market analysis and planned activities. Actual results may vary.

**Sales Projections (Year 1):**
Our sales goals are modest and targeted, reflecting the niche appeal of the game.

*   **Unit Price:** $7.99
*   **Platform Cut (Average):** ~15% (Itch.io's flexible cut averages lower than Steam's 30%)
*   **Net Revenue per Unit:** ~$6.80

*   **Launch Month (Itch.io):** 1,000 units = $6,800
*   **Months 2-6 (Itch.io):** 1,500 units (avg. 300/month) = $10,200
*   **Months 7-12 (Steam Launch + Sales):** 3,500 units = $23,800
*   **Total Year 1 Units:** 6,000
*   **Total Year 1 Gross Revenue:** **~$40,800**

This budget assumes a lean, remote team model with minimal overhead.

*   **Software and Licensing:**
    *   Development Tools (IDEs, etc.): $500
    *   Web Hosting & Domain: $200
    *   **Total:** $700
*   **Asset Creation:**
    *   Freelance Music/Sound Design: $2,000
    *   Freelance Cover Art/Promotional Art: $1,000
    *   **Total:** $3,000
*   **Marketing:**
    *   Digital Advertising (targeted): $500
    *   Conference/Festival Submission Fees: $300
    *   **Total:** $800
*   **Contingency Fund (15%):** ~$675

*   **Total Estimated Year 1 Expenses:** **~$5,175**

**Profitability Analysis:**
*   **Year 1 Gross Revenue:** $40,800
*   **Year 1 Expenses:** -$5,175
*   **Year 1 Estimated Profit (Pre-Tax):** **~$35,625**

This projection indicates a strong potential for profitability within the first year, providing a solid financial foundation for future projects or continued support for Stillnight Engine.

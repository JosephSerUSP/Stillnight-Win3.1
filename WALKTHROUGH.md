# Walkthrough: The Echoes of Aethelgard

*By ChronoSeeker99 | Version 1.0*

**INTRODUCTION**
Welcome to Stillnight. If you're here, you probably tried rushing to the stairs on Floor 1 and found them locked. This isn't a speedrun game; it's a puzzle box. Every floor requires a key, a seal, or a specific item to progress. This guide will help you find every key and break the loop.

---

### ZONE 1: THE ASHEN OUTSKIRTS

**Floor 1: The Silent Gate**
*   **The Lock:** The stairs are barred by a rusted iron gate.
*   **The Key:** **Rusted Key**.
*   **How to get it:** You need to hunt down the **Hollow Captain**. He looks like a normal skeleton but wears a tattered red cape (check the `Hollow Militia` enemies). He has a 100% drop rate for the key. Don't leave until you find him.
*   **Tip:** Use *Wind Blade* to clear the rats quickly.

**Floor 2: Crumbling Market**
*   **The Lock:** The "Merchant's Gate" blocks the path.
*   **The Key:** **Merchant Pass**.
*   **How to get it:** The local **Shop** sells it for 50 Gold. You'll need to grind a bit on this floor. Look for **Cinder Wisps**, they drop decent gold.
*   **Tip:** Don't buy the Iron Sword yet; save for the Pass.

**Floor 3: Cathedral of Dust**
*   **The Lock:** The Sanctum Door.
*   **The Key:** **Cathedral Key**.
*   **How to get it:** Defeat the floor boss, **Belltoll Gargoyle**.
*   **Strategy:** The Gargoyle is immune to Stun. Use *Ash Breath* (if you have the Tome) or just hit it hard. Watch out for its Sonic Shriek.

---

### ZONE 2: THE GILDED HALLS

**Floor 4: Hall of Mirrors**
*   **The Lock:** A shimmering barrier of glass.
*   **The Key:** **Mirror Shard**.
*   **How to get it:** There's a **Shattered Mirror** event somewhere on the map (looks like a `?` or a distinctive tile). Search it to find the shard.
*   **Warning:** Searching mirrors might spawn a **Mirror Mimic**. Be ready.

**Floor 5: The Royal Banquet**
*   **The Lock:** The bouncers (Ghostly Guards) won't let you pass.
*   **The Key:** **Banquet Invitation**.
*   **How to get it:** Find the **Ghostly Maid** NPC. She's usually hiding in a corner. Talk to her, and she'll slip you an invite if you listen to her story.

**Floor 6: The Throne Room**
*   **The Lock:** The Queen's Ward.
*   **The Key:** **Royal Seal**.
*   **How to get it:** Defeat **Queen Aeliana**.
*   **Strategy:** She heals herself. Use *Wail of Sorrow* or focused fire to out-damage her regen.

---

### ZONE 3: THE SPIRE OF TIME

**Floor 7: The Gearworks**
*   **The Lock:** A missing gear in the elevator mechanism.
*   **The Key:** **Clockwork Cog**.
*   **How to get it:** It's a rare drop from **Clockwork Sentinels**. You might have to fight 2 or 3 groups.
*   **Tip:** Sentinels have high physical defense. Magic users (Pixie/Wisp) are essential here.

**Floor 8: Fractured Observatory**
*   **The Lock:** The Star-Door requires a code.
*   **The Key:** **Starmap**.
*   **How to get it:** It's in a **Gold Treasure Chest**. This isn't random; there is exactly one Gold Chest on this floor containing the map.

**Floor 9: Precipice of Seconds**
*   **The Lock:** The Void Barrier.
*   **The Key:** **Void Key**.
*   **How to get it:** Defeat the Campaign Boss, **Malacor**.
*   **Strategy:** He uses *Time Stop*. Pray.

**Floor 10: The End**
*   Just walk to the hourglass and make your choice. You earned it.

---

**SECRET ITEMS**
*   **Rust-Eater:** Found on Floor 2. Cures the 'Rust' status (if implemented) or just heals you fully.
*   **Hourglass Sand:** Use this before the Malacor fight to boost your XP/Levels.
